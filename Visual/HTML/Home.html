You wake up in the middle of a dark hallway. You don't know how you got here, and you can't remember anything before this moment. The walls are cold, the air is thick, and there are no windows to show you the outside world. You're trapped in an unknown place, a maze with no end that seems determined to keep you here forever. There is only one way out, and to find it, you'll have to make crucial decisions at every turn and intersection. Each path could bring you closer to freedom or lead you deeper into the darkness.
The Forgotten Maze is a strange and twisted maze. Choose your path wisely and test your wits and survival instincts. Do you have what it takes to escape this place? The clock is ticking... your adventure begins now.
